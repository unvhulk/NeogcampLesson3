  1. DISCIPLINE EQUALS FREEDOM

    December,2021

    What is freedom? It might seem to an unsuspecting eye that we have freedom to do what we want and in a we do, but observing some more reveals that there are many more factors at work here. Our brain is looking to reduce the energy cost regularly and hence it develops patterns. These patterns or routines helps us reduce the processing of information so that we can use it somewhere far more suitable. As the time goes on, these repeating routines become habits. Habits are actually what shapes our life. Any skill , mastery over any crafty can be traced to consistent habits, but it works for the destructive patterns as well. Since our mind compulsively always seeks comfort and easy oxytocin which also releases dopamine which is the "craving" drug , feeding in the habit loop of cue , action and reward. Coming back to our question of freedom, habits dictate most of the people's lives.They are not choosing to do something 'consciously' rather they are compelled by their own mind to act in such ways.Mind are supposed to be our tools however we are being controlled. The keyword here is consciously.If we can control our habits consciously then we can be said to be in control but we cannot even control the always ongoing chatter of the mind. Most of us mistake compulsiveness with freedom, yet it is nothing but going through patterns set by our own minds, there is no choice.What is real freedom then? It is absolute control. Absolute control of one's own mind.Control can only be achieved by discipline. By doing what is necessary not what feels easy.Mind always wants to take the easy way out yet nothing can be done in comfort. A ship is safest on the dock but its meant to sail.

  2. What is God?

    December,2021

    In this blog I will go into the conceptualization of God by human beings.There are plethora of religions scattered on the planet right now.Many have risen and many have fell.Many are continuing into their struggle of supremacy of their God and their God is the only one, so much shackled by the believes integrated into their identity that they are unable to question anything objectively. There are various concepts of God that humans have held.One conceptualization of God is being the King of the world,owner of everything.But this concept seems to be incomplete as when we enquire that if he is the owner then who was the owner before him? If he was the owner from the start then who created them? Then the idea comes forth that he is the creator and omnipotent, the all powerful.But then the question rises if he is all powerful then why doesn't he stop the wrong doers? Why sinners florish and the good suffer? The answer given is that everyone suffers from the results of their actions. They claim cause and effect but if God is all powerful then why let anyone do wrong? Why allow bad people to exist? And if you observe every conceptualization till here is only the extention of human beings. A very powerful man ,very powerful woman, of different color,race, etc. with many hands and so on.Now there are some which go beyond this too. That which is beyond body , mind and senses and causes of all and effect of all is God. He is the ocean and we are just waves. He is the clay,we are the pot.Now this is a very interesting idea, just see, if we take clay away from pot does the pot remain? If we observe closely there was never a pot, it was only the clay. The pot is just a name given to a specific appearance of the clay.If you take out Gold from Gold necklace, there remains no necklace. Everything is just God, nothing else exists.Nothing else can exist and everything is just an appearance of God.Good and Bad, yin and yang are all appearances in the ocean.It's all appearance all unreal.Here unreal doesn't means false, it means it exist but we have superimposed something on the reality making it unreal.This idea of God encompasses every concept of God in it.
